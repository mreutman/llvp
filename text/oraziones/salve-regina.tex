% Hail, Queen, Mother of mercy, our life, sweetness, and our hope, hail. To you we cry, exiled children of Eve. To you we sigh, groaning and weeping in this valley of tears. Therefore, come then, our Advocate, turn those merciful eyes of yours toward us. And Jesus, the blessed fruit of your womb, show to us after this exile. O clement, O loving, O sweet Virgin Mary.
